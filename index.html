Hello and welcome to the CyberBored community! Thank you for stopping by and checking out my content. You will find here some of my projects, blogs, social media accounts, and resources to get started with Cybersecurity, Programming, and Networking.

As a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast, I am on a journey to understand a variety of technologies, attacks, security methods, and OPSEC for anonymity and privacy. Through this blog, I aim to not only document my personal journey but also provide valuable resources, insights, and discussions about the fascinating world of cybersecurity.

Here, you can find a variety of content and information, ranging from blogs to CTFs, projects, and videos. I will also be sharing on going projects and road maps as I advance and get more experience in the field.

Whether you’re a fellow cybersecurity enthusiast, a curious beginner, or someone looking to enhance their digital security, there’s something here for you. Check out my social medias where you can find a variety of content: youtube/@cyberbored and tikok/@cyberbored1

When I’m not involved in vulnerability research or projects, you might find me engaging in hands-on labs on platforms such as TryHackMe or HackTheBox.

Thank you for being a part of this cybersecurity journey. Let’s explore, learn, and secure together!
